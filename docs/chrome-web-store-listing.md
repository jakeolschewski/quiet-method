## Full Description (up to 16,000 characters)

```
QUIET METHOD — AI ATTENTION SHIELD

Your browser has been working against your brain. Quiet Method makes it work for you instead.

────────────────────────────────────────
WHAT QUIET METHOD DOES
────────────────────────────────────────

Quiet Method is an AI-powered Chrome extension that detects the early warning signs of digital overload — and intervenes with a single quiet nudge before you lose the next two hours.

It monitors three behavioral signals:

→ KEYSTROKE TIMING
The speed and rhythm of your typing changes measurably when you're stressed, scattered, or heading toward a spiral. Quiet Method learns your baseline and notices when things shift.

→ SCROLL VELOCITY
Frantic, fast, aimless scrolling is one of the clearest behavioral signals of cognitive overload. Not all fast scrolling means overload — Guard learns the difference between purposeful speed-reading and spiral behavior.

→ TAB SWITCHING FREQUENCY
Rapidly opening and switching tabs without completing tasks is one of the most reliable predictors of an approaching burnout spiral. Guard watches the velocity, not just the count.

When these signals converge — when your behavioral pattern matches the overload signature Guard has learned — you receive one quiet notification. No alarm. No popup blocking your screen. No productivity lecture. Just a single, timely nudge.

Then it's gone. You decide what to do next.

────────────────────────────────────────
100% LOCAL. COMPLETELY PRIVATE.
────────────────────────────────────────

This is not a small feature. It's the architecture.

Everything Quiet Method does — every behavioral measurement, every pattern match, every AI inference — happens on your device. Your keystroke timing, your scroll behavior, your tab patterns: none of it leaves your machine on the free tier.

There is no server receiving your data. There is no profile being built from your behavior. There is no third party monetizing your digital habits.

The free tier makes zero network requests for core functionality. You can verify this with any network monitoring tool.

Why did we build it this way? Because behavioral data — the way your mind moves through a workday — is genuinely sensitive information. It belongs to you. We built the architecture that way before it became a marketing angle, because we wouldn't want to use an extension built any other way.

────────────────────────────────────────
WHO QUIET METHOD IS FOR
────────────────────────────────────────

KNOWLEDGE WORKERS
If you work in a browser — writing, researching, designing, coding, managing — this is your environment. Quiet Method makes that environment smarter about your mental state.

PEOPLE WITH ADHD
The tab spiral is not a discipline problem. It's a feedback loop problem. Quiet Method interrupts the feedback loop early with an external signal — the kind of co-pilot cue that's harder to generate internally when your executive function is under pressure.

REMOTE WORKERS
In an office, other people could see when you were struggling. Remote work made the spiral invisible. Quiet Method makes it visible again — just to you.

PEOPLE WHO'VE BURNED OUT BEFORE
If you've burned out once, you know the pattern. Quiet Method catches you earlier in the cycle, before the pattern takes hold.

ANYONE WHO HAS 17 TABS OPEN RIGHT NOW
You know why you opened them. You also know you're not going back to most of them. Guard can help.

────────────────────────────────────────
HOW THE DETECTION WORKS
────────────────────────────────────────

Quiet Method uses on-device machine learning (powered by TensorFlow.js) to run behavioral pattern matching directly in your browser.

CALIBRATION PERIOD (First 24–48 hours):
When you first install Guard, it observes without intervening. It's learning your baseline — what your keystrokes look like when you're focused, how fast you normally scroll, what your typical tab-switching rhythm looks like when you're productive.

No two people have the same baseline. Guard doesn't use generic thresholds — it calibrates to you specifically.

DETECTION (After calibration):
Once calibrated, Guard watches for divergence from your baseline. It's not looking for "you opened too many tabs" — it's looking for the convergence of signals that, in your specific behavioral pattern, precedes a spiral.

The model improves over time. The longer you use Guard, the more precise the detection becomes.

INTERVENTION:
A single browser notification. No popup over your current page. No sound by default. Just a notification that says something like: "You've been showing overload signals for 8 minutes. Might be time for a pause."

It disappears in 10 seconds if you don't act on it. There's no countdown, no penalty for dismissing it, no shame mechanic if you ignore it.

────────────────────────────────────────

────────────────────────────────────────

The free tier of Quiet Method is not a trial. It's not a limited preview designed to frustrate you into upgrading. It's a complete, working product.

────────────────────────────────────────
QUIET METHOD PREMIUM — GO DEEPER
────────────────────────────────────────

For users who want to understand their patterns — not just detect them — Guard Premium turns on the analytics layer.

Premium includes everything in the free tier, plus:

✓ WEEKLY DEEP REPORTS
Full breakdown of your focus patterns. When do you peak? When do you reliably crash? What's your average recovery time after an intervention? What day of the week do you spiral most? Exported as a clean PDF.

✓ FOCUS FINGERPRINT
A personalized map of your peak focus windows, built from your actual behavioral data over time. Not generic productivity advice — your specific patterns.

✓ CROSS-DEVICE SYNC
Your calibrated behavioral model, encrypted client-side and synced to your other machines. Guard starts calibrated on every device, not from zero. Your encryption key stays with you — we cannot read your synced data.

✓ SMART SCHEDULING SUGGESTIONS
Guard notices when you schedule deep work in your low-focus windows (based on your own data) and flags it. Not a calendar app — just a heads-up when the timing doesn't match your biology.

✓ GOAL TRACKING AND STREAKS
Set your own focus goals. Guard tracks them without judgment, without public leaderboards, without gamification pressure.

Premium pricing: $4/month or $49 lifetime (all future features included).

────────────────────────────────────────
PERMISSIONS — WHAT WE REQUEST AND WHY
────────────────────────────────────────

We take permissions seriously. Here's exactly what Quiet Method requests and why:

TABS
Required to measure tab switching frequency — one of the three core overload signals. We do not read the URLs or content of your tabs. We measure switching velocity only.

STORAGE
Required to save your behavioral baseline and local reports to your device. This data never leaves your machine on the free tier.

ACTIVE TAB
Required to detect scroll speed and keystroke timing on the page you're currently viewing. We detect timing and velocity, not content. We cannot read the text you're typing.

NOTIFICATIONS
Required to send the intervention nudge when overload is detected.

What we deliberately did NOT request:
✗ <all_urls> (broad host permissions — we don't need to read your pages)
✗ history (we don't access your browsing history)
✗ cookies (we don't touch your cookies)
✗ webRequest (we don't intercept network traffic)

If you'd like to verify what data is being processed, the extension's behavior is auditable. Contact us at privacy@wedgemethod.com for details.

────────────────────────────────────────
BUILT BY WEDGE METHOD LLC
────────────────────────────────────────

Quiet Method is built by WEDGE METHOD LLC, a software company based in West Jordan, Utah.

The extension was built because the founder burned out twice while building software — and recognized that the behavioral signals of burnout were visible in browser activity long before the crash happened. If an external signal could interrupt the spiral earlier, the crash might not happen at all.

That's the hypothesis. Two years of building and testing later, the data says it's correct.

────────────────────────────────────────
FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
────────────────────────────────────────

Q: Does Quiet Method read the content of my web pages?
A: No. Guard detects timing and velocity patterns (how fast you type, how fast you scroll, how quickly you switch tabs). It cannot read text on your screen.

Q: Does Quiet Method track which websites I visit?
A: No. Guard does not access your browsing history and does not log URLs.

Q: Is the free tier really free forever?
A: Yes. The core burnout detection is free permanently. We don't time-limit it, feature-gate it to be frustrating, or plan to change this.

Q: How long does calibration take?
A: 24–48 hours for initial calibration. The model continues to improve over the first 2 weeks as it learns more of your behavioral data.

Q: What happens to my data if I uninstall?
A: All local data is deleted when you uninstall the extension. If you have Premium sync enabled, your data remains in your encrypted Supabase storage until you request deletion. See our privacy policy at quietguard.app/privacy.

Q: Does this work with Firefox or other browsers?
A: Currently Chrome and Chromium-based browsers only. Firefox support is on the roadmap.

Q: Is the source code available?
A: The detection engine is proprietary. The extension scaffolding and open-source components are MIT licensed. See our GitHub for what's publicly available.
```
