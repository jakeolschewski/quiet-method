Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. Introduction

WEDGE Method LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Quiet Method browser extension ("Extension"). This Privacy Policy explains how the Extension handles information when you install and use it. We are committed to protecting your privacy — which is why Quiet Method was designed from the ground up to process all data locally on your device.

Our registered address is available upon request at privacy@thewedgemethodai.com.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, store, or have access to any personal data.

The Extension processes the following signals entirely on your device to calculate your overload score:

  • Typing rhythm and velocity patterns
  • Scroll speed and direction
  • Number of open browser tabs
  • Calendar density (read-only access if granted)
  • Notification frequency

All processing happens locally using on-device AI models running in WebAssembly. No data is transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

3. Data Storage

All data generated by the Extension (overload scores, calm streak data, usage statistics, voice clone profiles) is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage and IndexedDB. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Is automatically deleted when you uninstall the Extension
  • Can be manually cleared at any time through the Extension settings

4. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with, send data to, or receive data from any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data processing services. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or any analytics platform
  • Advertising networks or tracking pixels
  • Cloud-based AI/ML services
  • Crash reporting services that transmit data
  • Any form of server-side data processing

5. Cross-Device Sync (Premium)

Premium users may opt into cross-device sync. If enabled:

  • Sync data is end-to-end encrypted before leaving your device
  • We use zero-knowledge encryption — we cannot decrypt your sync data
  • Sync can be disabled at any time, and all cloud-stored encrypted data will be permanently deleted within 24 hours
  • Sync data includes only settings preferences and aggregate calm streak counts — never raw behavioral signals

6. Browser Permissions

The Extension requests the following browser permissions:

  • tabs — To monitor tab count and detect tab switching patterns. We do not read tab content or URLs.
  • notifications — To manage notification muting during veil activation.
  • storage — To store your preferences and overload data locally.
  • idle — To detect when you're actively using the browser.

Optional permissions (requested only if you enable specific features):

  • calendar (read-only) — To assess calendar density for overload scoring.

7. Voice Clone Data (Premium)

The voice-cloned auto-reply feature processes your writing style to generate contextual replies. This voice profile:

  • Is generated and stored entirely on your device
  • Is never transmitted to any server
  • Can be deleted at any time through Extension settings
  • Uses on-device language models only

8. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly process data from children. Since we do not collect any data, this concern is moot in practice — but we state it here for completeness.

9. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access — You can view all data stored by the Extension at any time through the Extension settings panel. Since all data is on your device, you already have full access.
  • Right to rectification — You can modify your settings and recalibrate your overload baseline at any time.
  • Right to erasure — You can delete all Extension data through settings, or by uninstalling the Extension.
  • Right to data portability — The Extension provides an export function for your data in JSON format.
  • Right to object / restrict processing — You can pause or disable the Extension at any time.

Legal basis for processing: Since all data processing occurs locally on your device and no personal data is transmitted to us, the legal basis is your consent to install and use the Extension. You can withdraw consent at any time by uninstalling.

Data Protection Officer: Given that we do not process personal data, we have not appointed a DPO. For privacy inquiries, contact privacy@thewedgemethodai.com.

10. Your Rights Under CCPA

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with specific rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to know — We do not collect, sell, or share personal information. There is no data to disclose.
  • Right to delete — All data is on your device. Uninstall the Extension or use the clear data function in settings.
  • Right to opt-out of sale — We do not sell personal information. We have never sold personal information.
  • Right to non-discrimination — We do not discriminate against users who exercise their privacy rights.

Do Not Sell My Personal Information: We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal information for cross-contextual behavioral advertising.

11. International Data Transfers

Since no personal data is transmitted from your device, there are no international data transfers to disclose. The Extension operates entirely within your browser regardless of your geographic location.

12. Data Breach Notification

Because we do not store any user data on our servers, the risk of a data breach involving your personal information from the Extension is functionally zero. In the unlikely event of a security vulnerability in the Extension itself, we will:

  • Issue a security update through the Chrome Web Store within 72 hours
  • Publish a security advisory on our website
  • Notify affected users through the Extension's update mechanism

13. Payment Information

Premium and Lifetime purchases are processed through the Chrome Web Store's payment system or Stripe. We do not directly handle, store, or have access to your payment card details. Payment processing is subject to the respective processor's privacy policy.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. For material changes, we will notify users through the Extension's notification system. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance.
