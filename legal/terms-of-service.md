## 1. Description of Services

### 1.1 The Extension (Free Tier)

Quiet Method is a browser extension for Google Chrome and Chromium-based browsers that:

- Monitors browser behavioral signals (keystroke timing, scroll velocity, tab switching frequency) locally on your device
- Applies on-device machine learning to detect patterns associated with digital overload
- Delivers notification-based nudges when overload patterns are detected
- Generates local behavioral reports stored on your device

**The free tier of Quiet Method processes all data locally on your device. No behavioral data is transmitted to WEDGE METHOD or any third party as part of free-tier functionality.**

### 1.2 Quiet Method Premium

Quiet Method Premium is a paid subscription tier that includes all free-tier functionality plus:

- Weekly behavioral deep reports and Focus Fingerprint analytics
- Optional encrypted cross-device synchronization via Supabase infrastructure
- Smart focus scheduling suggestions
- Goal tracking and streak features
- Priority support

Premium is available as a monthly subscription ($4.00/month), annual subscription ($39.00/year), or one-time lifetime license ($49.00), subject to the pricing in effect at the time of purchase.
